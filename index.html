WHO WE ARE


We are one of the leading Garment Industry Training institutes in Sri Lanka established in 1999 which provides and opportunity for school leavers as well as job seekers to enter the apparel industry and to learn for the basic.

We are registered under Tertiary Vocational Education Commission(TVEC) and have obtained recognition from the TVEC by receiving the approval to conduct NVQ(National Vocational Qualifications) courses in the industry. All certificates are issued by the TVEC after assessing their competency.

Our syllabus is made with the consultation of highly qualified and experienced professionals employed in leading apparel companies with the technical knowledge and approved by TVEC.




Our Vision


To open your pathway to the garment Industry by making you a dynamic individual who has the ability to full fill the employer needs to suit the current industry and to make Sri Lanka the HOME OF APPERAL INDUSTRY.


Our Mission


To make our Students more efficient reliable individuals by giving them the proper education, practical knowledge and essential skills to full fill the carder requirements in the garment industry locally and internationally.
